# Content Guide

Use this as a reference when planning the site structure. Find the client's business type, check which sections apply, and copy the relevant checklist into `discovery.md`.

---

## Restaurant / Bar / Cafe

### Must Have
- [ ] **Hero** — full-bleed photo, name, tagline, CTA (reserve a table / view menu)
- [ ] **About** — brief story of the place, chef, founding year, atmosphere
- [ ] **Menu** — ideally a live, updatable section (not a PDF); categories (starters, mains, desserts, drinks)
- [ ] **Gallery** — food photos, interior, ambiance (min. 6–8 images)
- [ ] **Location & Hours** — embedded Google Map, address, opening hours per day
- [ ] **Contact** — phone number, email, social links

### Recommended
- [ ] **Table Booking** — embedded widget (OpenTable, TheFork, Resy) or custom form
- [ ] **Reviews** — pull from Google or TripAdvisor; 3–5 highlighted quotes with stars
- [ ] **Special Events** — seasonal menus, private dining, live music nights
- [ ] **Takeaway / Delivery** — link to Uber Eats, Glovo, or own ordering system

### Optional
- [ ] **Team** — chef profile, front-of-house intro
- [ ] **Press** — "as seen in" logos or quotes from food publications
- [ ] **Gift Cards**
- [ ] **Newsletter signup**

### Content to Request from Client
- High-res food and interior photos (min. 10)
- Full menu with prices
- Logo (SVG preferred)
- Google Business profile link (for reviews embed)
- Booking platform they use (or preference)
- Opening hours
- Story / about text (or bullet points to write from)

## Retail Shop (physical store)

### Must Have
- [ ] **Hero** — store vibe, main product category, CTA (visit us / shop online)
- [ ] **Products / Categories** — showcase key product lines
- [ ] **Location & Hours** — map, address, hours
- [ ] **Contact**

### Recommended
- [ ] **About / Our Story** — brand values, origin, what makes them different
- [ ] **Gallery** — store interior, products in context
- [ ] **Reviews** — Google reviews or testimonials
- [ ] **Promotions / Sales** — seasonal offers, current deals

### Optional
- [ ] **Online Store** — full e-commerce (significant scope increase)
- [ ] **Loyalty Program** — info or signup
- [ ] **Newsletter / Offers signup**
- [ ] **Brand Partners / Suppliers**

### Content to Request from Client
- Product photos (clean background + lifestyle)
- Logo and brand colors
- Opening hours
- Any current promotions

## Service Business (plumber, electrician, cleaner, etc.)

### Must Have
- [ ] **Hero** — clear headline stating what they do and where, CTA (get a quote / call now)
- [ ] **Services** — list of services with short descriptions
- [ ] **Why Choose Us** — 3–4 differentiators (licensed, fast response, local, etc.)
- [ ] **Contact / Quote Form** — phone prominent, form for non-urgent requests
- [ ] **Service Area** — cities/zones they cover

### Recommended
- [ ] **Reviews / Testimonials** — Google reviews or client quotes
- [ ] **About** — who they are, years of experience, certifications
- [ ] **Gallery / Before & After** — photos of completed jobs
- [ ] **FAQ** — common questions about pricing, timing, process

### Optional
- [ ] **Pricing** — at least indicative ranges (builds trust)
- [ ] **Emergency CTA** — sticky phone button for urgent calls
- [ ] **Certifications / Badges**
- [ ] **Blog** — local SEO content (e.g. "how to fix X in [city]")

### Content to Request from Client
- Service list with descriptions
- Photos of work / jobs completed
- Certifications or license numbers (if they want to display)
- Service area (list of cities/zones)
- Response time / availability (e.g. "available 7 days")

## Professional / Freelancer (lawyer, accountant, consultant, etc.)

### Must Have
- [ ] **Hero** — who they are, specialty, CTA (book a consultation / contact)
- [ ] **Services / Practice Areas** — clear list with short descriptions
- [ ] **About** — credentials, experience, approach
- [ ] **Contact** — form, phone, office address

### Recommended
- [ ] **Testimonials / Case Results** — social proof (check legal restrictions on claims)
- [ ] **FAQ** — common questions about their process, fees, first appointment
- [ ] **Process / How It Works** — step-by-step of working with them
- [ ] **Blog / Resources** — builds authority and SEO

### Optional
- [ ] **Team** — if multi-person firm
- [ ] **Press / Publications**
- [ ] **Online Booking** — Calendly or similar
- [ ] **Pricing / Packages** — especially for consultants

### Content to Request from Client
- Professional bio + photo (high quality, neutral background)
- List of services / specializations
- Certifications, bar number, professional memberships
- Any testimonials they have

## Beauty & Wellness (salon, spa, barber, tattoo, etc.)

### Must Have
- [ ] **Hero** — atmosphere photo, name, CTA (book now)
- [ ] **Services & Pricing** — full service menu with prices
- [ ] **Booking** — online booking widget (Fresha, Treatwell, Vagaro, or custom)
- [ ] **Gallery** — work portfolio (haircuts, treatments, tattoos, etc.)
- [ ] **Location & Hours**
- [ ] **Contact**

### Recommended
- [ ] **Team / Artists** — profiles with their specialty and portfolio
- [ ] **Reviews** — Google or platform reviews
- [ ] **About** — story, values, what makes the space special

### Optional
- [ ] **Products for Sale** — retail products used in treatments
- [ ] **Gift Vouchers**
- [ ] **Loyalty / Membership**
- [ ] **Aftercare / Blog**

### Content to Request from Client
- Portfolio photos (high quality, consistent style)
- Full service list with prices
- Team photos and bios
- Booking platform they use
- Opening hours

---

## Notes

- **Booking widgets** always require a third-party account — confirm with the client which platform they use or want to use before committing to an integration.
- **Review embeds** (Google, TripAdvisor) may require API access or manual curation — clarify during discovery.
- **Menu updates** and **gallery updates** are the most common post-launch change requests — factor into pricing and handoff.
